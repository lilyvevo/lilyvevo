Reading

Admittedly, I love writing but tend to fall short when it comes to reading. I'm hooked on fantasy stories- pass me a copy of some magical tale and I'll tear through it- but I abandon, like every other genre.

However, this year I fell in love with one of the most amazing books I've ever read- and it was a piece of realistic fiction! And a love story! These two genres I really disliked, but when I read this book I loved it.

Aristotle and Dante Discover the Secrets of the Universe is a remarkable book. Seriously, you have to read it. It's funny, it's heart-wrenching, and also finally depicts a grumpy teenager without being stereotypical- yay. It's about identity, and also about ~love.~ Seriously please read this book, it's amazing.
